1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how PeopleRate ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information when you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email, username, password, full name
  • Profile Information: Professional details, bio, profile photo (optional)
  • Review Content: Reviews, ratings, comments you post
  • Communications: Messages you send to us or other users

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Cookies: Session cookies, preference cookies, analytics cookies
  • Log Data: Access times, error logs, performance data

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Social Media: If you connect LinkedIn, Instagram, etc.
  • Public Sources: Publicly available professional information

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Core Services

  • Create and manage your account
  • Display your public profile and reviews
  • Enable search and discovery features
  • Process and display user-generated content

3.2 Communication

  • Send account notifications and updates
  • Respond to support requests
  • Send platform updates and announcements
  • Email verification and password resets

3.3 Improvement and Analytics

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Monitor platform performance and errors
  • Conduct research and testing
  • Develop new features and services

3.4 Safety and Security

  • Detect and prevent fraud and abuse
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Moderate content for compliance
  • Protect against security threats

4. Information Sharing

4.1 Public Information

The following information is PUBLIC and visible to all users:

  • Your username (not your real name or email)
  • Reviews and ratings you post
  • Public profile information (if you claim a profile)
  • Average ratings and review statistics

4.2 Private Information

The following information is PRIVATE and NOT publicly shared:

  • Your email address
  • Your full name (unless you choose to display it)
  • Your password (encrypted)
  • Your IP address and device information
  • Private messages and support communications

4.3 Third-Party Sharing

We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Hosting, email, analytics (under strict agreements)
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or legal process
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest
  • Password hashing with bcrypt
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Automated backup systems

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Correction

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Update your profile and preferences
  • Download your data (data portability)

6.2 Deletion

You can request deletion of:

  • Your account and associated data
  • Specific reviews you've posted
  • Your public profile (if claimed)

Note: Some information may be retained for legal compliance or legitimate business purposes.

6.3 Marketing Communications

You can opt out of:

  • Marketing emails (unsubscribe link in every email)
  • Platform announcements (account settings)
  • Push notifications (device settings)

Note: You cannot opt out of essential service communications (e.g., password resets, legal notices).

6.4 Cookie Preferences

You can control cookies through:

  • Browser settings (block or delete cookies)
  • Our cookie banner (accept/reject)
  • Opt-out of analytics cookies

7. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Most data deleted within 30 days
  • Legal Requirements: Some data retained for legal compliance (e.g., 7 years for financial records)
  • Public Reviews: May be retained in anonymized form for platform integrity

8. Children's Privacy

PeopleRate is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover we've collected data from a child, we will delete it promptly.

9. International Data Transfers

PeopleRate is based in the United States. If you access our service from outside the U.S., your data will be transferred to and processed in the U.S. By using our service, you consent to this transfer.

10. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Legal Basis: We process data based on consent, contract performance, or legitimate interests
  • Right to Object: Object to processing for direct marketing or legitimate interests
  • Right to Restriction: Request restricted processing in certain circumstances
  • Data Protection Officer: Contact privacy@peoplerate.com
  • Supervisory Authority: You can lodge complaints with your local data protection authority

11. CCPA Compliance (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under CCPA:

  • Right to Know: What personal information we collect, use, and share
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of sale of personal information (note: we don't sell data)
  • Non-Discrimination: We won't discriminate against you for exercising your rights

12. Cookies Policy
